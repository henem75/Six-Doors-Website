About Us

Six Doors Orient is a global advisory firm headquartered in Singapore. We help corporates and innovators thrive at the intersection of technology, digital transformation, and sustainability. Large organizations often struggle to pivot quickly, while startups may lack the networks and resources to scale across borders. We bridge this gap by sourcing, integrating, and commercializing innovations within corporate value chains—supported by structured project management and training.

For corporates: we accelerate transformation by bringing vetted, market-ready solutions. For startups: we enable access to markets, distribution, and financing networks—without compromising growth.
